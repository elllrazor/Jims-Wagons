Jim's Wagons

Handcrafted Gypsy Wagons - Made with Tradition & Passion

About Jim and His Craft

Jim is a nearly 70-year-old craftsman and bricklayer who has been building stunning gypsy wagons for over 10 years. Growing up, Jim’s father was a Gypsy, and making these wagons brings him closer to his family roots. Every wagon Jim creates is unique and a testament to his dedication and skill, taking him up to six months to complete just one. Jim’s space allows him to work on a single wagon at a time, so each wagon is truly one-of-a-kind.

Our Wagons

Each wagon is carefully crafted with tradition and heart. Jim creates one wagon at a time, and once it’s finished, it’s available for sale until the right person comes along to appreciate its beauty. Due to the time and care it takes, each wagon comes as is, ready to find a new home without customization options. When you buy a wagon from Jim, you’re purchasing a piece of heritage.

Interested in Purchasing?

If you’d like to inquire about Jim’s latest completed wagon, please reach out! Note that wagons are available on a first-come, first-serve basis due to the time it takes to craft each one.
