About Jim and His Craft

Jim, a nearly 70-year-old craftsman and bricklayer, has been building beautiful, handcrafted gypsy wagons for over 10 years. Growing up, Jim’s father was a Gypsy, and creating these wagons brings him closer to his roots and family heritage. With each wagon he builds, Jim pours in his passion, skill, and a lifetime of experience. Every wagon is a tribute to the traditions of his past and the joy of working with his hands.

Our Wagons

At Jim's Wagons, we offer both custom-built gypsy wagons and a selection of finished wagons. Each wagon can be tailored to your specifications, including size, color, and materials. Whether you're looking for a decorative piece or a fully functional wagon, Jim’s dedication to quality craftsmanship ensures that each wagon is built to last.
